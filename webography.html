Attention Span is defined as the amount of time someone can concentrate on one task without being distracted. Recent studies have grappled with the average time of attention spans nowadays but there is a stark difference because one relates to when digital devices are in use and the other is just simply doing a task. Students and the Digital Age Why Does it Matter?
Having Media Literacy is an important skill to have and it is a must to be taught in classrooms today. Teaching Media literacy allows the students to apply their critical thinking skills, intake information and come to a competent conclusion. It must be shown that this skill is something that can and should be used in all life situations that it may be needed. It can allow for powerful insights and connections to be made and explored because it's basically just the art of asking questions to come to a fair conclusion. Written by Faith Rogow With information being so easily accessible in today’s world, it is also just as easy to read some misinformation on the internet. Having digital media literacy allows you to use the internet but also understand and discern the information that is being presented. The internet allows a lot of people to be experts in fields that they weren’t even students in. You have to know how to fact check and use real life clues to reach a proper conclusion. This skill is very important to have today as Artificial Intelligence is overtaking everything. Written by Brad Smith
